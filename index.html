Biography

Xianjing Han is currently a Ph.D candidate at Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Liqiang Nie and Dr. Xuemeng Song. She is a Visiting Scholar in Department of Computer Science at Texas State University, in 2019-2020, under the supervision of Dr. Yan Yan. She received her B.E. degree from Northeastern University, China, in 2017. Her works focus on the fashion analysis and video understanding.
